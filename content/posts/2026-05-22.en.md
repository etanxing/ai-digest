## 1. Google DeepMind Accelerator program launches in Asia Pacific
Google DeepMind has launched its Accelerator program in the Asia Pacific region, with a focus on tackling environmental risks using [AI](https://deepmind.google/blog/were-launching-the-google-deepmind-accelerator-program-in-asia-pacific-to-tackle-environmental-risks/). The program aims to support startups and researchers in the region in developing innovative solutions to pressing environmental challenges. By providing access to [Google DeepMind's](https://deepmind.google/blog/were-launching-the-google-deepmind-accelerator-program-in-asia-pacific-to-tackle-environmental-risks/) expertise and resources, the Accelerator program seeks to accelerate the development of [AI-powered](https://deepmind.google/blog/were-launching-the-google-deepmind-accelerator-program-in-asia-pacific-to-tackle-environmental-risks/) solutions for environmental sustainability.

The launch of the Google DeepMind Accelerator program in Asia Pacific marks a significant expansion of the company's efforts in the region, and underscores the growing importance of [AI](https://deepmind.google/blog/were-launching-the-google-deepmind-accelerator-program-in-asia-pacific-to-tackle-environmental-risks/) in addressing environmental challenges. With the Asia Pacific region being home to some of the world's most populous and rapidly developing countries, the program has the potential to make a significant impact on environmental sustainability in the region. According to [Google DeepMind](https://deepmind.google/blog/were-launching-the-google-deepmind-accelerator-program-in-asia-pacific-to-tackle-environmental-risks/), the Accelerator program will provide support to startups and researchers in the region through a combination of funding, mentorship, and access to [AI](https://deepmind.google/blog/were-launching-the-google-deepmind-accelerator-program-in-asia-pacific-to-tackle-environmental-risks/) expertise and resources.

The program's focus on environmental sustainability is in line with [Google DeepMind's](https://deepmind.google/blog/were-launching-the-google-deepmind-accelerator-program-in-asia-pacific-to-tackle-environmental-risks/) broader mission to use [AI](https://deepmind.google/blog/were-launching-the-google-deepmind-accelerator-program-in-asia-pacific-to-tackle-environmental-risks/) to benefit society and the environment. By supporting the development of innovative [AI-powered](https://deepmind.google/blog/were-launching-the-google-deepmind-accelerator-program-in-asia-pacific-to-tackle-environmental-risks/) solutions for environmental sustainability, the Accelerator program aims to contribute to a more sustainable and environmentally conscious future.
